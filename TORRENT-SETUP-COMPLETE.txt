## 🎓 KEY INSIGHTS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Phase 1 (Seeding) is Critical**
   - Don't skip straight to downloading
   - This week builds trust with trackers
   - Your 72h seed commitment = better peer cooperation
   - M admins notice and may forgive H&Rs

2. **Ratio = Currency**
   - On private trackers, ratio is your account health
   - High ratio = priority seeding, no H&R bans
   - Low ratio = warnings, temporary bans, donor-only status
   - This ecosystem is designed to prevent ratio collapse

3. **Seeding > Downloading**
   - Upload speed is your bottleneck (likely <10 MB/s)
   - Never download faster than you can upload
   - This config enforces 5 downloads max concurrent
   - Allows 20 upload slots for simultaneous seed capacity

4. **Community Matters**
   - IRC is where admins announce rules changes
   - Staying connected shows engagement
   - Reaching out about H&Rs shows good faith
   - Some trackers have appeal processes
